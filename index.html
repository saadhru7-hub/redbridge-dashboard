Lesson Observation Rubric
Redbridge International School · Cambridge-aligned · 6 criteria · 4-point scale
Criterion 4 — Exemplary 3 — Proficient 2 — Developing 1 — Unsatisfactory
1. Lesson Planning & Alignment
4
Fully aligned with curriculum objectives; clear, measurable learning outcomes; pacing supports all ability levels.
3
Clear learning outcomes; most activities aligned; pacing appropriate for majority.
2
Partial alignment; outcomes unclear or inconsistently communicated; pacing uneven.
1
No clear objectives; activities misaligned; ineffective time management.
2. Student Engagement
4
All or nearly all students actively engaged; varied strategies; high enthusiasm and participation.
3
Most students engaged; some variation in teaching methods; participation monitored.
2
Inconsistent engagement; limited strategies; some off-task behavior not addressed.
1
Majority disengaged; no engagement strategies; poor learning climate.
3. Questioning & Assessment
4
Consistent higher-order questioning; strong formative assessment; immediate, specific feedback.
3
Mix of question types; some higher-order thinking; regular checks for understanding.
2
Mostly recall-based questions; limited assessment; general feedback.
1
Minimal or ineffective questioning; no assessment for learning; no feedback.
4. Classroom Management
4
Highly structured, positive environment; routines embedded; smooth transitions; proactive behaviour management.
3
Orderly environment; routines established; minor disruptions handled effectively.
2
Inconsistent routines; some disruption; responses not always effective.
1
Frequent disruptions; lack of routines; unsafe or unproductive environment.
5. Differentiation & Inclusion
4
Clear differentiation for SEND, EAL, and high achievers; all learners supported effectively.
3
Some differentiation; most needs met; basic accommodations in place.
2
Limited differentiation; some learners unsupported.
1
No differentiation; significant learner needs unmet.
6. Subject Knowledge
4
Expert subject knowledge; deep explanations; extends learning beyond curriculum.
3
Secure knowledge; accurate teaching; minor gaps in depth.
2
Basic understanding; occasional inaccuracies; limited extension.
1
Weak subject knowledge; errors uncorrected; misconceptions reinforced.
Scoring Guide: 20–24 Excellent 14–19 Satisfactory 8–13 Requires Development Below 8 Formal Improvement Plan
